Questions from Our Customers

How fast can Pinnacle secure a truck for an urgent load?

Most time-critical loads are covered within minutes because we pull from a pre-vetted network that already handles expedited work. You’re not waiting for someone to negotiate with random load-board carriers during a crisis.

What types of expedited options do you provide?

We match the mode to your actual deadline and budget. Options include dedicated truckload, team drivers, hotshot vehicles, sprinter vans, and LTL-expedited for smaller urgent shipments. The goal is to use the fastest viable option, not the most expensive one.

Can I see the shipment in real time once it’s moving?

Yes. You get live tracking, location updates, and proactive alerts if anything changes. You won’t have to chase status updates or sit in the dark wondering where the truck is.

What if my urgent freight is high-value or fragile?

Expedited carriers are selected based on experience with sensitive freight, not just speed. We only dispatch vetted carriers who know how to handle high-value, high-risk, or damage-prone shipments under tight deadlines.

Can Pinnacle handle last-minute requests outside normal business hours?

Yes. Most urgent loads happen outside the 9–5 window. Our expedited support covers nights, weekends, and off-hours scenarios where your internal team usually scrambles.

How do you keep costs in check on expedited shipments?

Speed doesn’t have to mean waste. We prevent unnecessary premiums by:

- Using the right mode for the deadline
- Avoiding over-sizing the vehicle
- Dispatching vetted carriers instead of load-board roulette
- Catching preventable bottlenecks before they turn into “rush” emergencies

Most teams overspend on expedited because they lack a framework. We help build one.
