LTL Flow: Smart Freight Management

End-to-end LTL bid sourcing and management platform that optimizes your freight costs and streamlines carrier selection
Get Started

What is LTL Flow?

Intelligent Freight Sourcing

LTL Flow is a comprehensive platform that manages the complete lifecycle of LTL bid sourcing. From analyzing shipment history to optimizing carrier selection, our system leverages data-driven insights to reduce costs and improve efficiency across your entire freight network.

Automated Workflow Management

Streamline your LTL procurement with automated shipment aggregation, lane analysis, and bid management. Our platform integrates seamlessly with your existing systems, providing real-time visibility and control over your entire sourcing process.

Powerful Features for Modern Logistics

Shipment History Analysis

Import and analyze historical shipment data to identify patterns, optimize lanes, and make informed decisions about carrier selection.

Lane Aggregation

Automatically aggregate shipments into lane summaries, visualizing demand across geographic regions and time periods for strategic planning.

Rate Optimization

Leverage SMC3 RatewareXL integration to get accurate LTL pricing and optimize discount structures across weight breaks and lanes.

Carrier Management

Manage carrier relationships, track performance metrics, and ensure compliance with your shipping requirements and service standards.

Bid Management

Streamline the bidding process with automated RFP generation, bid comparison tools, and scenario analysis for optimal carrier selection.

Project Tracking

Monitor sourcing projects from inception to completion with comprehensive reporting and analytics on cost savings and performance metrics.
