Delivery-First Document Timing

Most cross-border delays happen because DODAs, DODA Consolidado, and U.S. Entry docs get issued too early. Pinnacle reverses that logic. We build the document schedule around your confirmed delivery window so everything stays valid and on time when the truck reaches the border.

Visibility for Every Step

Factories, brokers, and consignees rarely see the full lifecycle of a shipment in one place. Pinnacle consolidates status, document timing, delivery windows, and truck location into a shared view so both sides know what is happening without guessing.

Powerful Features for Modern Cross-Border Freight

Delivery-Aligned Document Control

Time DODA, DODA Consolidado, and U.S. Entry to match the actual delivery appointment and crossing plan.

B1 Driver Options

When freight is floor loaded or damage sensitive, B1 drivers move direct from Mexico to your U.S. facility without risky transloads.

Smart Transload Strategy

If transloading is required, Pinnacle uses vetted cross-dock partners positioned just past the border to minimize handling and risk.

Customs Liaison Support

We coordinate with your customs broker or introduce trusted partners. Our team includes former brokerage staff who ensure both sides stay aligned.

Real Communication Across Borders

We run bilingual operations using channels that get answered. Mexico teams get rapid WhatsApp updates. U.S. consignees get calls, email updates, or a shared dashboard.

Shared Document and Status Visibility

Factory and consignee see the same information in a live dashboard: document issue dates, expiry dates, appointment status, and real-time truck movement.

Proven Results

Delivery‑first document planning

We start with the U.S. delivery time and carrier capacity, then time your DODA, DODA Consolidado, and U.S. Entry so docs are valid when you actually plan to cross – not days before.

B1 drivers and smart transload choices

For floor‑loaded and damage‑sensitive freight like furniture, we specialize in B1 drivers that go direct from Mexico to your U.S. facility, avoiding costly, risky transloads. When transload makes sense, we use vetted cross‑dock partners just past the border.

Customs liaison support

Our team includes people who’ve worked inside customs brokerages. We coordinate with your existing broker or connect you with trusted partners, helping Mexico exporters get properly set up and keeping both sides aligned.
