How LTL Shipping Works

LTL carriers consolidate freight from multiple shippers into one truck, creating efficient routes through hub-and-spoke networks. Your shipment moves through terminals where it's sorted and combined with other freight heading in the same direction. This system provides:

Lower shipping costs compared to full truckload
Reduced environmental impact through shared resources
Reliable transit times with established carrier networks
Advanced tracking and visibility throughout the journey
Professional handling at each terminal stop
