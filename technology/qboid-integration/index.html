QBOID Integration

Seamless Order-to-Shipment Mapping with Mobile Dimensioning Technology
Get StartedWatch Video

Revolutionary Mobile Dimensioning

QBOID is the first truly mobile, handheld dimensioning solution designed for efficient warehouse and supply chain operations. Our integration enables seamless order-to-shipment mapping, eliminating manual measurement processes and reducing costly shipping errors.

Powered by advanced 3D computer vision technology, QBOID captures accurate dimensions for parcels, pallets, and irregular-shaped items in seconds. The data seamlessly flows into your existing warehouse management systems, creating a complete digital record from order receipt to final shipment.
Capture dimensions in under 3 seconds per item
