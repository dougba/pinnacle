Ceiling-Mounted Pallet Dimensioning for High-Volume LTL Docks.

High-Speed Overhead Pallet Dimensioning

Powerful Integration Features

Everything you need for automated pallet dimensioning and clean data flow at high-volume docks.

Order Receipt

FS5000 turns a single dock spot into a dedicated measurement lane. Operators move pallets onto the scale platform as part of their normal flow, and the overhead sensors capture dims, weight, and an image automatically. Processing time per pallet drops dramatically while accuracy improves.

System Integration

FreightSnap integrates with LTL Flow and many leading TMS, WMS, and ERP platforms. Once connected, measurement data flows straight into rating and routing logic, eliminating clipboard notes, spreadsheets, and manual entry at the office.

Shipment Execution

Because every pallet is measured the same way at the same station, you build shipments, rate them, and negotiate with carriers on standardized data. That reduces reweigh/reclass risk and makes billing and QBR conversations fact-based instead of opinion-based.
