Our Story

Pinnacle was built to solve the freight problems most providers avoid. From day one, we focused on the categories where logistics breaks most often: big, fragile freight, floor-loaded imports, multi-stop truckload, cross-border Mexico, and high-variance LTL. These flows require more than a broker – they require a partner willing to do the hard, detailed work that keeps your freight moving without surprises.

We started small but with a clear mission: streamline logistics through programs that are tailored, transparent, and reliable. Today, Pinnacle is a tech-enabled, values-driven transportation partner that combines decades of operational experience with tools like LTL Flow, QBOID, FreightSnap, Pinnacle Consolidate, and BorderView to reduce damage, eliminate variances, and improve timing across every mode.
We design and run end-to-end programs for furniture, home goods, consumer products, automotive, and industrial shippers. Whether it is drayage across 100+ miles, 20-stop truckload routes, Mexico direct TL or LTL, or complex consolidation from floor-loaded LTL, our team takes on the challenges that big-name brokerages avoid.
Our mission is simple:
Provide freight programs that deliver accuracy, visibility, and peace of mind across every shipment.

Customer-First Approach

Every decision is guided by what is best for your freight, your deadlines, and your customer experience. Clients stay with us because execution never takes a back seat to sales.

Relationship-Driven

We build long-term partnerships through transparency, real communication, and consistent performance. Our team acts as an extension of yours.
