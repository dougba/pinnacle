Home Furnishings Logistics That Protects Fragile, High-Mix Freight

We have spent years working alongside home décor, lighting, cabinet, mirror, rug, and fixture brands to identify and solve their most pressing freight and damage challenges.

Fragile, High-Mix Freight

High-SKU assortments with delicate materials create constant damage risk. Mixed cartons and irregular packaging move through many hands, raising the likelihood of breakage and hidden damage. Pinnacle reduces this risk with category-specific prep standards, QA checklists, and carrier choices tuned to fragile home goods.

Floor-Loaded Containers and Excess Handling

Floor-loaded imports packed with mixed SKUs lead to labor spikes, overload stages, and more touches. Without drayage timing and controlled unload/reload workflows, fragile cartons suffer avoidable damage. Pinnacle plans container timing and structured unload/reload processes so cartons move with fewer, better-managed touches.

LTL Variances and Compliance Issues

Reweighs, reclasses, and accessorials are common with décor and fixtures. Retail compliance failures at DCs and stores add chargebacks, delays, and disruption. Pinnacle uses LTL Flow, QBOID, and standardized prep to send properly documented pallets that meet retailer expectations and reduce LTL surprises. We’ll guarantee you no reweigh reclass fees, ever again.

Unclear Landed Cost and Reverse Logistics

Unplanned TL, LTL, and parcel moves make cost per unit unpredictable. Misroutes, mis-picks, and late damage reporting cause expensive, chaotic reverse logistics. Pinnacle structures forward and reverse flows, tracks cost per SKU, and gives you clear reporting so returns and astray freight stay under control.
