Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our food & beverage solutions
How do you ensure temperature control throughout transit?

We partner exclusively with certified refrigerated carriers equipped with real-time temperature monitoring devices. You receive automatic alerts if temperatures deviate from your specified range, and all temperature data is logged and accessible for compliance purposes.

What happens if a delivery is delayed?

Our system automatically monitors transit times and triggers contingency plans for potential delays. You receive proactive notifications with alternative solutions before issues impact your delivery schedule. We also maintain a network of backup carriers for emergency situations.

Can you handle both frozen and refrigerated products?

Yes, we support all temperature ranges from frozen (-20°F to 0°F), refrigerated (32°F to 40°F), to climate-controlled (50°F to 70°F). Each shipment can have customized temperature requirements automatically communicated to carriers.

How do you help with food safety compliance?

Our platform automatically generates and maintains all required documentation including temperature logs, carrier certifications, and chain-of-custody records. We ensure compliance with FDA, USDA, FSMA, and other relevant food safety regulations.

What's your coverage area for refrigerated transport?

We have a nationwide network of certified refrigerated carriers covering all 48 continental states. We also partner with specialized carriers for Alaska, Hawaii, and Canada to ensure comprehensive coverage.
